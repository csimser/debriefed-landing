Eval parser. Upload your evaluation or performance report and instantly translate military write-ups into civilian-ready bullet points. FITREP, EVAL, NCOER, OER, EPR -- scan it and translate it.

◆

Dictionary-first architecture. 33,000+ entries translate military jargon before the AI ever touches it. Cheaper to run, more accurate results.

◆

Full resume pipeline. Resume builder, cover letters, LinkedIn optimizer, job match analysis, federal resume support.

◆

Ready to deploy. Next.js, Supabase, Vercel. Schema dump, seed data, and setup docs included. Fork it and run it.

I built this because transitioning service members deserve tools that work without a price tag attached. If your organization can put this in front of the people who need it, please do. Credit the original project and send me a link to what you build.
